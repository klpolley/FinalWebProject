Welcome to Mentor Match

It's our mission to match you with the right person for your study needs.

Mentor Match strives to help struggling student athletes find academic success by connecting them with peer mentors excelling in certain courses. Founded in 2018 by Sonya Gattus, Audrey Lang, and Kate Polley, Mentor Match has a 110% success rate of improving student athletes' grades, raising their self-esteem, and aiding to create lifelong (or at least college career-long) friendships.

Some of our most successful peer mentors include Professor Pancake, Mouse Cop, and Little Russian Lady. Successes at Mentor Match are, forgive the pun, unmatchable, and the positive outcomes can last a lifetime.
